When water collects on a road, a vehicle can hydroplane. Hydroplaning is a condition in which directional control is partially or totally lost because of water on the roadway. It is caused when the tires lose contact with the road surface and have little or no traction. Braking and steering may not be possible while hydroplaning. Hydroplaning 2
