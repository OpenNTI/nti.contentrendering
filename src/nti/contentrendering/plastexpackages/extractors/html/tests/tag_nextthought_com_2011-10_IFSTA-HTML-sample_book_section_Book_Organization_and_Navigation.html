Book Organization and Navigation

Your homepage displays when you first log in and contains the books required to complete your training. Click on a book to begin.

Your book will open on the first page. Notice at the top of the page are navigational tools: the first icon opens the table of contents and search. Click on it to preview the entire book outline. Click on a chapter, section, or subsection to navigate directly to that content.
